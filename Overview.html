Origami

Overview Lyrics

About the Song

Origami is a song by The Rare Occasions. The imagery of folding into origami is used as a metaphor for insecurity, self-expression, and personal growth.

Theme

The song explores identity, anxiety, and the desire to be accepted while learning to embrace oneself.

My Thoughts

I really like this song. The lyrics are very relatable and the melody is catchy. It has a calming vibe that makes it a great song to listen to.
